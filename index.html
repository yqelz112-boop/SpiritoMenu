About Spirito

Artisanal Pizza Crafted with Passion

Founded in 2020, Spirito brings authentic American-style pizza to Al-Muqla. Our master pizzaiolos combine traditional techniques with locally-sourced ingredients to create unforgettable flavors.

Every pizza is made to order, with dough fermented for 48 hours to develop the perfect texture and flavor. We believe in the art of pizza making and the joy it brings to our community.
